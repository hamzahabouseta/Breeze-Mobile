Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join?

Joining is easy! All you need to do is sign up by providing some of your basic information, such as your name, address and email. Once you sign up, we will send you your sim card which you will use to access our network. Insert the sim card into your phone and you are ready to go.

Can I use my existing number?

Yes, switching is made easy.

What devices are compatible with the Breeze Mobile network?

Any device that is currently compatible with Rogers Communication network will work fine with ours.

Do I have to unlock my phone in order to use Breeze Mobile?

Yes. You must unlock your device in order to use the Breeze Mobile service.

Can I use Breeze Mobile outside of Ontario? Outside of Canada?

Yes. Breeze Mobile network is available all across Canada, from coast to coast. However, if you use Breeze Mobile outside of Canada, fees may apply (yet to be determined).
