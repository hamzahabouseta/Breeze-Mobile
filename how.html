How Breeze Works

Breeze Mobile's mission sounds impossible, but it's not. Here's how it works, it's really simple.

No Towers.

We currently do not operate our own cell towers. We plan to "piggyback" off another, larger carrier's network, which means we'll pay them to let us use their towers to provide service to our customers.
